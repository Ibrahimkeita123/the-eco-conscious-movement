Welcome to the Eco-Conscious Movement!

We’re a passionate team dedicated to creating a sustainable future by advocating for sustainable climate change adaptation & mitigation, plastic pollution control, environmental justice, proper waste management, and innovative solutions like the circular economy and waste valorization.

Through public education and data-driven initiatives, we tackle terrestrial and aquatic pollution challenges head-on. Our work is rooted in scientific research and public education, with the goal of inspiring meaningful change at both individual and community levels.

We believe that by understanding our impact, we can create a healthier planet for generations to come.

Our Thematic Areas

Climate Action – A Collective National Task

The mokwa flood that happened of recent did real bad damage to properties and lives. Nigeria is highly vulnerable to climate change impacts such as flooding.

Raising a Generation of Environmental Stewards

With over 60% of Nigeria’s population under 25, our youth are the greatest asset to champion a greener future. Raising environmental stewards is not just timely — it’s essential for national development.

Plastic Pollution: Taking Collective Action

Nigeria generates ~2.5 million tonnes of plastic waste annually. The River Niger is among the 10 most plastic-polluting rivers in the world.

We advocate for system transformation: reducing sachet water dependency, improving waste management, and enforcing sanitation laws.

Environmental Injustice

From oil spills in the Niger Delta to toxic dumps near poor communities — those who contribute the least to pollution often suffer the most.

We are here listening for the deaf, speaking for the dumb, and fighting for the weak.

Waterbodies & Wetlands Protection

Nigeria’s rivers, lakes, lagoons, and wetlands are under severe threat from pollution and poor management. We promote climate-resilient agricultural practices and indigenous adaptation strategies to protect these vital resources for future generations.

Some of Our Works

In our relentless pursuit to combat plastic pollution, the Eco-Conscious Movement has spearheaded numerous impactful initiatives across Nigeria. Our campaigns have successfully mobilized communities, schools, and local governments to take decisive action against plastic waste. Through educational workshops, we have raised awareness about the detrimental effects of plastic pollution on our ecosystems and public health. Our advocacy efforts have led to the implementation of stricter waste management policies in several regions, promoting recycling and reducing single-use plastics. Additionally, our community clean-up drives have resulted in the removal of tons of plastic waste from rivers, beaches, and urban areas, restoring natural habitats and enhancing the quality of life for residents. These efforts not only highlight our commitment to environmental stewardship but also inspire collective action towards a cleaner, greener Nigeria.>

Our school sensitisation programs have been pivotal in educating the younger generation about the importance of environmental conservation. By engaging students through interactive sessions, we have fostered a sense of responsibility and empowerment among youth to become active participants in the fight against plastic pollution. These programs have equipped students with knowledge about sustainable practices, encouraging them to advocate for change within their families and communities. Through art competitions, recycling projects, and environmental clubs, we have nurtured a culture of sustainability that extends beyond the classroom. The enthusiasm and commitment demonstrated by these young environmental stewards underscore the transformative power of education in shaping a more eco-conscious society.
