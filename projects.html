Projects

Project galleries are horizontally scrollable. When focused, use left and right arrow keys to navigate the gallery.

Sustainability sensitisation for farmers using Asa Dam, Ilorin

Promotes responsible water use and sustainable farming practices, ensuring the long-term health of Asa Dam and local food security.

Street Clean-up Exercise

| Immediately improves local sanitation and aesthetics, reduces litter that can block drainage, and encourages community ownership of public spaces.

Awareness Outreach for High School Students

Fosters the next generation of eco-conscious leaders by educating young adults on climate change, resource management, and active community participation.

River Clean-up Exercise

Protects aquatic ecosystems and biodiversity, prevents plastic pollution from flowing downstream, and enhances the overall health of the watershed.

Open Market Campaign

Engages the public directly on reducing plastic waste, promoting reusable alternatives, and encouraging local businesses to adopt greener packaging.

Visit to Plastic Recovery Centre

Raises awareness of the circular economy by demonstrating the value of waste, encouraging better sorting, and supporting local recycling jobs.

Climate Action, tree planting and donation to schools.

Combats climate change through carbon sequestration (tree planting), enhances local green spaces, and supports environmental education in schools with necessary resources.|
